How to Get Rid of Pesky Pests the Natural Way

Posted by on 2023-11-19

Getting rid of pesky pests can be a drag, but you don't have to resort to harsh chemicals or expensive solutions! There are many natural ways to rid your home and garden of pests.

First, make sure that all food is stored securely in airtight containers and dispose of garbage regularly. This can help discourage insects and rodents from entering your home in search for food. Also, keep the area around your house neat and free of debris and vegetation (this includes compost piles which should be kept away from the house!).

Next, introduce beneficial bugs into your garden like ladybugs or praying mantises. These bugs naturally prey on plant-eating insects making them an effective solution without having to use any pesticides or other chemical products. Additionally, if you have a pond or water feature in your yard it's a great idea to introduce fish as they will feed on mosquito larvae helping reduce the number of these annoying flying insects buzzing around!

Furthermore, planting certain herbs like mints and lavender around the perimeter of your property can help repel some types of insects as well as create a pleasant aroma in your garden. Along with this, using certain essential oils such as neem oil may also deter some bugs from getting close to your plants.

If none of these natural methods work you could always hire an exterminator who uses organic methods instead (which still require caution).

In conclusion, there are plenty of natural ways to get rid off pesky pests without having to resort to harsh chemicals! With some patience and creativity you'll soon be able see results while still being environmentally friendly. So why not give it a try? You've got nothing left too lose!
