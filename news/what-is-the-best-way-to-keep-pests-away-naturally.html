What is the Best Way to Keep Pests Away Naturally?

Posted by on 2023-11-19

Pest control can be an annoying and expensive process, but there are ways to keep pests away naturally! Not only is it more cost-effective, but it can also be better for the environment. One of the best ways to prevent pest infestation is by keeping your home and yard free from clutter. This means removing any piles of leaves, wood, or other debris that may provide a nice place for bugs and rodents to hide. Additionally, make sure to regularly clean up around pet areas and dispose of pet waste immediately (as unpleasant as this task may be).

Another great way to deter pests is through planting certain flowers and herbs in your garden. For example, marigolds are known to repel mosquitoes while lavender keeps moths away. You can even try adding some garlic or chives near doorways; these plants have strong odors that many bugs don't like!

Finally, you should inspect all possible entry points into your home such as windows and doors; seal off any cracks or crevices with caulk to prevent critters from getting in. You may also consider installing screens on doorways and windows so insects won't be able to get inside.

In conclusion, there are plenty of natural solutions available when it comes to keeping pests away. If you take the time wholisticly examine your property for potential hiding spots for critters and invest in some natural repellents, then you'll likely have a much easier time keeping pests at bay! But always remember: prevention is key!
